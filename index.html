Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform

What is Dhansetu?
Dhansetu is a skill-based trading competition platform where traders can showcase their skills in simulated market environments. Participants pay an entry fee to join challenges, and top performers win cash rewards based on their trading performance.
Is real money trading involved?
No, all trading activities on Dhansetu are conducted in a simulated environment using virtual funds. We do not manage real client funds or provide investment services.
How are rewards distributed?
Cash rewards are distributed via UPI or bank transfer to top performers within 24 hours of challenge completion. All rewards are subject to applicable TDS deductions under Indian tax laws.
What markets can I trade?
Our simulator provides access to major Indian and global markets including NSE equities, commodities, currencies, and international indices. The specific instruments available depend on the challenge tier.
Is Dhansetu registered with SEBI?
Dhansetu operates as a skill-based competition platform and does not provide investment advisory or portfolio management services. As such, SEBI registration is not applicable to our business model.
